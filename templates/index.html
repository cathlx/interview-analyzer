About

This tool allows you to analyze the interview and determine whether the person giving it has external or internal reference (or mixed, that's possible, too). Person with internal reference will rely on their own judgement to make decisions and evaluate themselves, while someone with external reference uses objective outside data, such as feedback and performance metrics.
